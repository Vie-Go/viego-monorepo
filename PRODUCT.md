# Product

<!-- impeccable:product-schema 1 -->

## Platform

adaptive

## Users
Cultural explorers and travelers gamifying their journey across Vietnam's provinces.

## Product Purpose
VieGo is an interactive cultural exploration app that enables users to discover, unlock, and collect Vietnam's rich regional heritage, traditions, and local beats province by province.

## Positioning
A gamified regional discovery platform combining an interactive Vietnam map, daily cultural streaks, regional trivia, and rich audio-visual heritage collections that make exploring Vietnamese culture immersive and habitual.

## Operating Context
Mobile application used on-the-go while traveling or during daily discovery rituals. Users interact via map exploration, unlocking province cards, tracking progress streaks, and sharing cultural milestones.

## Capabilities and Constraints
- **Capabilities**: Interactive Vietnam map with province and ward metadata (`provinces-metadata.json`, `vietnam-provinces.geojson`), multi-language support (Vietnamese, English, etc.), user authentication (Email, Google, Facebook, Zalo), province unlocking mechanism, daily streak counters, cultural trivia/beats audio player, dark/light theme switching.
- **Technical Constraints**: Target native mobile app components (iOS / Android adaptive) with responsive mobile ergonomics.
- **Terminology**: Province unlocking, Streaks, Cultural Beats, Regional Heritage, Unlocked status.

## Brand Commitments
- **Name**: VieGo
- **Palette**: Signature deep crimson red (`#BE382A`), warm vibrant yellow/gold (`#F2B72F`), warm off-white canvas (`#EBE5DC` / `#F6F3EE`), sleek dark theme (`#151011`).
- **Typography**: Urbanist font family with bold geometric weights (800 / 700 / 600).
- **Tone & Voice**: Warm, vibrant, culturally proud, encouraging, and playful.

## Product Principles
1. **Cultural Pride First**: Celebrate Vietnam's rich regional diversity through authentic, vivid, and respectfully crafted visuals and content.
2. **Gamified Joy**: Turn cultural learning and travel discovery into an engaging daily ritual with rewarding unlock moments.
3. **Tactile Mobile Ergonomics**: Design for fluid single-handed mobile navigation with micro-animations and crisp tactile feedback.
4. **Seamless Multi-region Experience**: Provide frictionless language switching, accessible themes, and instant visual feedback on progress.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Ensure high color contrast ratios for both light (`#BE382A` on `#FFFFFF`) and dark themes, multi-language support (Vietnamese & English priority), clear touch targets (>44px), and readable typography across screen sizes.
